Welcome to Maxcare Hospital

Established in 1984 and with a capacity of over 50 beds, Maxcare Hospital built to provide quality and world-class care to patients throughout their recovery journey.

Maxcare provides the highest standard of clinical expertise and nursing care. It prides with the latest technology and hospital facilities. It began as a vision of Dr. Prem Kumar and a group of medical doctors to provide high-quality and low-cost healthcare service to the common people.

At Maxcare Hospitals, we are devoted towards clinical excellence, patient centricity, and ethical practices.
